# ADR 0033 - Feedback Is Product Feedback, Not Clinical Outcome

Status: Accepted

Context: The MVP is synthetic and unapproved.

Decision: Feedback asks about understandability, explanation usefulness, usability and governance clarity only.

Consequences: Feedback evidence does not collect clinical correctness, identifiers or operational case details.

Alternatives considered: clinical outcome feedback. Rejected because the model is unapproved and synthetic-only.
