# ADR 0050: Champion-Challenger Does Not Imply Promotion

## Status

Accepted.

## Context

Champion-challenger comparison is evidence for review, not an approval workflow.

## Decision

Gate pass may support a recommendation for registration review, but it does not approve, activate, deploy, retire, roll back or replace the champion.

## Consequences

The representative workflow may retain the champion. Human governance remains required for any future lifecycle mutation.
