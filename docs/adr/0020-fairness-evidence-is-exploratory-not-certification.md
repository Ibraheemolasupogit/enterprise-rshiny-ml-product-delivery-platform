# ADR 0020: Fairness Evidence Is Exploratory Not Certification

Status: Accepted

## Context

Synthetic subgroup data is small and not representative of real populations.

## Decision

Milestone 6 reports subgroup metrics with minimum-size suppression and explicit limitations.

## Consequences

Fairness evidence supports review but is not legal, clinical, or production certification.

## Alternatives Considered

Omitting subgroup checks was rejected. Treating synthetic metrics as certification was rejected.
