# Fairness Assessment

Milestone 6 performs exploratory subgroup checks across sex, age band, and deprivation group. Metrics include row count, positive rate, predicted positive rate, mean predicted probability, recall, specificity, precision, false-positive rate, false-negative rate, and Brier score where sample size permits.

Groups below the configured minimum size are suppressed and labelled unreliable. The evidence is descriptive synthetic evidence only and is not legal, clinical, or fairness certification. Observed disparities should guide future review rather than automatically approve or reject a candidate.
